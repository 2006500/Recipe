Simple Omelette Recipe
An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegatables,or meats.
Preparation time
  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Prepartion: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes

    • Ingredeints
    2-3 large eggs
    salt, to taste
    Pepper, to taste
    1 tablespoon of butter or oil
    Optional filling:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

    Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed. You can add tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
    Heat the pan:Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
    Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
    Add filling(optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still sightly
    runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
    Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the filling.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it into a plate.
    Enjoy: Serve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
